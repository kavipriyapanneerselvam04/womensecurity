Self Defense Guide

User: -
Learn practical awareness, escape steps, and travel readiness.

Escape First

Use voice, balance, and space. The goal is to break contact and reach safety fast.

See Notice exits and people.
Alert Shout "Stop" or "Help".
Fight Only to create space.
Exit Move to light and crowd.

4 Quick Moves

1
Ready stance
One leg back. Hands up. Chin down.
2
Break the grab
Turn toward the thumb gap and pull free.
3
Hit and move
Eyes, nose, groin, shin, then leave.
4
Use what you carry
Bag, bottle, umbrella, torch as barrier.

Travel Smart

Before leaving: Share route and charge phone.
While moving: Choose bright roads and verified transport.
While waiting: Stay near shops, families, or guards.
If uneasy: Trust your instinct and leave early.

Practice In 5 Minutes

1 min: Ready stance in front of a mirror.
1 min: Loud voice practice: "Stop", "Back off", "Help".
1 min: Open alarm, torch, and phone fast.
2 min: Step back, turn, and move away quickly.
